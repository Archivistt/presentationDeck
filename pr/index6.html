Methodology

ATEC Workflow

Operational Process for Meal Claiming

Step 01

Arrive

Student physically approaches the meal claiming station at the canteen.

Step 02

Tap

Student taps their registered RFID card on the reader for instant verification.

Step 03

Ensure

System validates identity, eligibility, and available credit balance in real-time.

Step 04

Claim

Meal is dispensed, credit is deducted, and the transaction is digitally logged.
