Problem Statement

Excessive Administrative Workload

The PSAS Department staff dedicates approximately 8 hours daily solely to physically sorting, organizing, and manually recording coupon submissions one by one.

Operational Bottlenecks

Manual verification creates long queues during peak meal times, reducing the time students have to eat and rest.

Resource Wastage

Recurring costs for vellum paper and laminating film, exacerbated by frequent issues of missing or misplaced physical coupons.

Security & Unauthorized Claims

Lack of effective validation mechanisms makes the system vulnerable to unauthorized meal claiming and identity misrepresentation.

Compromised Data Integrity

Manual record-keeping results in fragmented records and human errors, making accurate auditing and reporting nearly impossible.
